  2. Statement from advisor:

    This paper presents the first systematic method for evaluating the performance of a gradual typing system. The result required an application of the method to a practical gradual typing system and a new kind of graph to visualize the results. The paper also demonstrates that a sampling technique can approximate the results of a full evaluation (exponentially large) with a linear number of samples. Ben produced most of the results and prose with guidance from all co-authors.
