  3. Paragraph from advisor justifying the choice of the Committee:

    Ben's work explores new directions in both the theory and implementation of mixed-typed languages; the committee therefore incorporates a wide range of expertise. Vitek has experience building systems and conducting rigorous performance evaluation. Ahmed is an expert in the theory of language interoperability. Krishnamurthi leads the development of Pyret, a pedagogic mixed-typed language. Henglein has done foundational work combining static and dynamic typing. Tobin-Hochstadt built the language (Typed Racket) that Ben's implementation work extends.
