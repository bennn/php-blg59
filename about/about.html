About

I'm Ben. Ph.D. student at Northeastern University. I am interested in constructions.

The plan for now is to work on abstraction and assurance under the guidance of Amal Ahmed and Matthias Felleisen. I want to be able to refactor implementations and to have more than a unit test suite to convince me the codebase is still safe.

I've also been working on object-oriented type systems with Ross Tate. We're working together with friends in industry to bring the joys of a decidable type system to a sad lonely world. I know Tony Hoare wrote that the willingness of a computer to "accept almost any sequence of instructions and make sense of them at its own level" is something that should be "cherished", but I'm a fan of syntactic discipline. Now if only I could figure out what an object is.
